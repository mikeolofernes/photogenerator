Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, completely free. The default mode uses Pollinations AI, a free public API that requires no account or key. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or credit limits.
Pollinations AI is a free, open-access image generation service powered by FLUX. It accepts requests from anyone without authentication — similar to how you can open an image URL in a browser. Your style selection is turned into an optimized prompt and sent to their API.
Yes — when you upload a photo, the app uses face-preserving image-to-image AI (InstructPix2Pix via a free HuggingFace Space) that edits your actual photo to apply professional attire and background while keeping your face intact. No token or account required.
Your uploaded photo is sent to the timbrooks/instruct-pix2pix public Space on HuggingFace. This model uses an instruction like "make this person wear a navy suit" to edit your photo — changing clothing and background while preserving the face geometry and identity. No API key required; the Space is publicly accessible.
Your compressed photo is sent directly to the HuggingFace public Space for inference — it is not stored on any server we control. Generated thumbnails are saved in your browser's localStorage only. This is a fully static, serverless app with no database or backend.
Pollinations AI typically responds in 5–15 seconds. HuggingFace inference can take 20–60 seconds, and if the model is cold (not recently used) the first request may take up to 2 minutes while it loads. Subsequent requests are faster.
Pollinations AI's outputs are available under a permissive license suitable for personal and professional use. HuggingFace model outputs are subject to each model's license. For LinkedIn, resumes, and company profiles, you're free to use these headshots.
