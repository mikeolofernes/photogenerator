❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

HeadshotAI uses advanced image-to-image AI (powered by fal.ai) to transform your casual photo into a professional headshot. The AI analyzes your facial features, preserves your identity, and applies the chosen professional style with studio-quality lighting and background.
This app is completely free and open-source. You only pay for your own fal.ai API usage — typically a few cents per image. New accounts include free credits to get started.
Your key is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is sent directly from your browser to fal.ai — never through any third-party server. We recommend creating a scoped key on the fal.ai dashboard limited to only the models this app uses, and setting a spending limit. Never commit a real API key to a public GitHub repository.
Use a clear, well-lit, front-facing photo where your face is clearly visible. Aim for at least 512×512px. Avoid blurry images, extreme angles, hats covering your face, or heavy filters. Natural or soft indoor lighting works best.
The AI is designed to preserve your facial identity. It changes your clothing, background, and lighting to match the chosen style while keeping your face recognizable. Minor lighting enhancements may be applied.
Most headshots generate in 15–40 seconds, depending on server load. fal.ai's GPU-accelerated infrastructure is optimized for fast inference.
Generated images are yours to use, subject to fal.ai's terms of service. Since you're generating a headshot of yourself, the result is suitable for personal and professional use (LinkedIn, resumes, company profiles, etc.).
This app stores thumbnails of recent generations in your browser's localStorage only. Your photos are sent to fal.ai per their privacy policy. No data is stored on any server we operate — this is a fully static, serverless application.
